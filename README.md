# Media Careers India

A curated collection of resources, guides and community links for people pursuing careers in media across India — journalism, broadcasting, digital media, PR, content creation and related fields.

## Goals
- Collect learning resources (courses, books, tutorials)
- Share internship and job leads
- Curate interview and portfolio tips specific to the Indian media industry
- Build a community for mentoring and peer feedback

## Structure
- resources/ — curated links and descriptions
- internships/ — known internship listings and how to apply
- interviews/ — tips and sample questions
- templates/ — portfolio/CV templates and cover-letter examples
